INTRODUCTION

GOAL QUEST FOUNDATION is a Community Based Organization committed to sustainability, social changes & youth empowerment. We are devoted to engaging the youths in matters of environmental responsibility. We aim to empower young minds with the knowledge and tools necessary to become responsible environmental stewards.

OUR PILLARS

1. Youth Empowerment:

-Empowering the next generation through education, mentorship and skill development.
-Creating opportunities for young individuals to unlock their full potential and contribute to their communities.

2. Environmental Conservation:

-Initiatives focused on sustainable practices, reforestation and reducing our ecological footprint.
-Collaborating with communities to raise awareness and implement eco-friendly solutions.

3. Mental Health Awareness:

-Breaking the stigma surrounding mental health through advocacy and educational programs.
-Providing resources and support for individuals facing mental health challenges.

OUR MISSION


To inspire people to enhance health and overcome hunger and hardships.

OUR VISION


To empower a world to which the less fortunate will have the power to uplift themselves out of poverty.
